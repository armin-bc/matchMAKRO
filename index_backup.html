About AI Kitchen Helper

Welcome to AI Kitchen Helper! This app is designed to help you discover new recipes using the ingredients you already have. Simply tell us what's in your pantry by typing a list or uploading a picture, and our AI will suggest delicious meal ideas.

We've designed this app to be a community resource. When our AI generates a new recipe, it's saved for others to discover, and you can save your favorites to your personal list. This creates a growing library of recipes for everyone to enjoy.
